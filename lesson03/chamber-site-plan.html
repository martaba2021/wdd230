Site Name - South Lyon Chamber of Commerce

The reason I choose this site name and city, it is because I currently reside in the City of South Lyon in Michigan. It is a small but growing community.

Site Purpose

South Lyon Chamber of Commerce main purpose is to work and server together with local businesses. The main goal of this chamber is to promote the economic growth of it's members through member services, and engaging in activites that improves both businesses and the community.

Scenarios

  1. What activity is the Chamber of Commerce currently working on to promote it's mission?
  2. What has been the economic impact of the Chamber of Commerce to it's members?
  3. How often board members meet for planning and provide status of their internal affairs?
  4. What are the requirements to become a member?
